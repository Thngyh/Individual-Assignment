Project Overview

A preview individual assignment from Internet Application, Semester A/2025.

To discuss the emerging internet trend of the Internet of Things (IoT) for food preparation and safety in the Food and Beverage (F&B) industry. The site's purpose is to

present well-explained, relevant, and informative content that clearly demonstrates how this trend is shaping the F&B industry.

Key Features

  • Responsive Design validated across Desktop, Tab, and Phone views.
  • In-depth analysis of IoT's transformative impact in the F&B industry.
  • Discussion of automated monitoring systems and digital workflows.
  • Focus on achieving improved efficiency, reduced waste, and enhanced food safety protocols through technology.

Technologies Used

The final webpage was assessed on technical implementation and was required to have proper CSS styling and interactivity. It was built to be a visually appealing and responsive web design.
